ABOUT

Mechanica is the technical festival of Mechanical Engineering, presented by Mechanical Engineering Association (MEA), the largest of its kind at IIT Madras. The fest was started as a humble attempt to strengthen the foundations of mechanical students, and, pave a way for them to understand theory in the context of real life, with a glistening variety of contests and events.

Why Become a CA

The Campus Ambassador Program is a platform for you to improve your communication, marketing and social skills. It offers a whole range of activities which give you a hands-on experience in digital marketing, publicity and event management. The incentives include:

Internship & Passport

  • Top 5 CAs willget free accommodation during Mechanica.
  • Top 3 CAs stand a chance to win cash prizes worth Rs 3000/-

Certificate of Appreciation

  • Best CA will be awarded a special certificate of appreciation from HOD.

Improve Your Skills

  • The ability to motivate and lead a team.
  • Excellent Communication and 'People Skills'.
