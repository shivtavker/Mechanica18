Guidelines for Accomodation in Mechanica
  • Accommodation will be provided from 12.00pm on 9th March, 2018 to 11.00pm on 11thMarch, 2018. These timings will be followed strictly.
  • The payment can be done in prior online through our payment gateway.
  • Online Payment:
    • Accommodation charge will be Rs.300 per person per day.
    • An extra Rs.100 per person will be collected before check-in as caution deposit which will be refunded after the fest.
  • Offline Payment:
    • Accommodation charge will be Rs.350 per person per day.
    • Caution deposit remains the same.
  • Accommodation will be charged as per the 12am-12am basis and not 24hr basis.
  • The accommodation will be given to boys and girls separately at boys hostel and girls hostel.
  • In case of any cancellation of accommodation booked online already, only half of the money will be reimbursed.
  • A confirmation mail will be sent to the participant once the accommodation is approved. The participant has to carry a copy of the confirmation mail.
  • Accommodation will be strictly provided only to the students and not to anybody accompanying the students.
  • Accommodation will be given on a sharing basis in common rooms which can accommodate 20-25 people. Single occupancy rooms will not be provided at any cost.
  • We will be providing the students with a set of mattress, pillow and blanket, which should be safely returned before checking out.
  • Accommodation fee is for providing rooms only. Food is not included.
